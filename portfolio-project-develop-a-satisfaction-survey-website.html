Developing a Satisfaction Survey Website for Asia Technology College

Septemder 01SUN, 2024

During my studies at Asia Technology College, I had the opportunity to develop a satisfaction survey website. The website was designed to evaluate three important student projects: the "Multi-Purpose Vending Machine," the "Cricket Protein Cookies," and the "Laser Staff Walking Stick." Each of these projects showcased different skills and innovation from the students.

For the development of this website, I used HTML, CSS, and JavaScript as the main technologies to ensure ease of use and efficient functionality. The website was designed with simplicity in mind, making it user-friendly and accessible. It allows users to easily evaluate the projects and provide their feedback quickly and effectively.

The website features pages where users can select the project they want to review. These include the " Multi-Purpose Vending Machine," designed for user convenience; the "Cricket Protein Cookies," a nutritional product made from crickets; and the "Laser Staff Walking Stick," designed to assist individuals with visual impairments. Users can rate each project based on their satisfaction and submit their opinions or suggestions through Google Sheets for data collection and further analysis.

The website is now available online at https://asia-lb.web.app. Developing this website not only enhanced my technical skills but also contributed to the students' ability to present their work to the public and receive valuable feedback for improvement in the future.
